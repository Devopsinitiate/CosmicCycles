About Cosmic Cycles

What is This App?

This web application is a tool designed to help you understand and navigate the various cyclical influences in your life. It is based on the principles outlined in the book "Self-Mastery and Fate with the Cycles of Life" by H. Spencer Lewis, a profound work that explores the hidden rhythms of the universe and their effect on our lives.

The core idea is that everything in the universe, from the smallest atom to the largest galaxy, operates in cycles. By understanding these cycles, we can work in harmony with them to achieve greater success, happiness, and self-mastery.

The Cycles of Life

The application calculates and displays several key cycles, each with its own unique characteristics and influences:

  • The Daily Cycle: This cycle is divided into seven periods, each lasting approximately 3 hours and 25 minutes. It governs the subtle energies of the day and can be used to determine the most propitious times for various activities.
  • The Yearly Cycle: This cycle begins on your birthday and is divided into seven periods of approximately 52 days each. It reveals the major themes and influences you will encounter throughout the year.
  • The Human Life Cycle: This is a larger cycle that divides our lives into seven-year periods, each with its own developmental focus.
  • The Business Cycle: Similar to the yearly cycle, but applied to the start date of a business or a major project.
  • The Soul Cycle: This cycle is based on the solar year and reveals the deeper, soul-level influences at play in our lives.

For Further Research

For those who wish to delve deeper into the principles behind this application, we highly recommend reading "Self-Mastery and Fate with the Cycles of Life" by H. Spencer Lewis. The book provides a comprehensive explanation of the cycles and how to use them to your advantage.

The information presented in this application is based on the charts and tables found in the book, particularly in chapters 5, 6, 7, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15 and 16.
